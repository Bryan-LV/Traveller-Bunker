What To Do In
Cameron Highlands

Cameron Highlands is best known for it's beautiful landscape, tea plantations, and outdoor adventures. In CH, you can trek 10 different paths that each have their own stunning views. You can visit the famous BOH tea plantation where you get incredible views of Malaysia's rolling hill's along with delicious tea and food at BOH's tea center. There is the mossy forest which is an incredibly rare formation found in Cameron Highlands. CH also has a lot of other activities such as farms and gardens that are open to visitors.
